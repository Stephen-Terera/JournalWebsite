CPD JOURNAL

My time at Nottingham trent university start off a bit strange.I was unable to get to campus in time for the start of the term which meant back to online lessons just like my last year of high school.This affected my CPD to some extent because things like part time jobs and Digital Horizons talks were out of the question and meant it was a bit harder to get points.It was harder to get points but not impossible.I just had to look for things that I thought were intersting and beneficial.

The corona virus forced a number of operations to move online and as the situation continues to get better some things have stayed online.This meant that I could still attend employer sessions and online workshops and things like that. Playing sport at the university counts towards CPD points and although I was not there to play sport I still trained and went to the gym.This counted for zero of my points but I found it helped me clear my mind and I would feel so much better after a gym session or a run.
